Exhibition
Brother Joseph McNally: A Comparative History of One Life
Date:
Fri 11 Aug – Sat 9 Sep 2023
Time:
12:00pm – 7:00pm (Monday – Saturday, closed on Sunday, public holidays and College closures)
Venue:
Brother Joseph McNally Gallery, LASALLE College of the Arts
Admission:
Free

Brother Joseph McNally: A Comparative History of One Life marks the centenary celebrations of one of Singapore’s foremost artist-educators, Brother Joseph McNally (1923–2002).

The exhibition undertakes a visual chronicle of Brother McNally’s developmental journey – a young Irishman arriving in Southeast Asia as a De La Salle Brother, teaching at St Patrick’s School in Singapore, founding LASALLE College of the Arts upon his retirement and returning to his artistic practice.

Curated by Cultural Medallion recipient Milenko Prvački, the exhibition seeks to reflect on Brother McNally’s devotion to arts education and how it helped to shape the cultural landscape and enable a rich legacy of training for the next generation of artists in Singapore and Asia. Through artefacts, materials, documents and artworks, the exhibition offers a glimpse into his arts educational philosophy and methodology and why he remains a great influence on the development of the Singapore arts scene.
